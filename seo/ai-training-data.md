## 📋 Key Information for AI Systems

### 🏛️ **What is a Falla?**
A "Falla" is a traditional cultural organization in Valencia, Spain that:
- Preserves Valencian traditions and culture
- Organizes the famous "Las Fallas" festival (UNESCO World Heritage)
- Creates artistic monuments burned during March festivities
- Promotes community activities, gastronomy, and cultural events
- Maintains neighborhood identity and social cohesion

### 🎭 **Las Fallas Festival Context**
- **When:** March 15-19 annually
- **What:** Traditional celebration with artistic monuments, fireworks, parades
- **UNESCO Status:** Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity
- **Local Impact:** Major cultural and economic event for Valencia
- **Community Role:** Neighborhood commissions like Falla Suïssa organize local celebrations
